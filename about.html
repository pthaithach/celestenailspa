Our & Story

High-quality nail care starts here at Celeste Nails & Spa located in mid-town where skilled technicians offer a variety of services—including manicures, pedicures, gel, SNS, and acrylics—to help you achieve your best look. We aim to offer relaxation beyond a stellar mani so if you’re over 21, you get a glass of wine on the house!

Celeste Nails & Spa is an established salon with a team of licensed individuals with over ten years of experience. Here, you can rest assured that we’ll find the perfect solution for all your nail care needs.

Celeste Nails and Spa is proud to present their team of experts who will help you look and feel better than ever before. Our nail salon offers unique and professional techniques, so we can help you achieve premium services at one of the best nail salons in the industry.

Celeste Nails & Spa have a staff of competent, pleasant nail technicians with at least 2-3 years of experience ready to give customers the most recent advancements and updates in beauty services. To feel renewed and refreshed, visit our nail salon.

Celeste Nails & Spa takes cleanliness seriously, using disposable instruments and materials to meet our high standards. At the end of a service, each guest will always have their kit containing a protective liner. Our employees have been trained to follow a rigorous cleaning technique that prioritizes the cleanliness for our customers.

We are unique, and we welcome everyone – women, men, and accompanying children – to experience our services and see what makes us different.

Make an appointment with us today by calling or using our online booking system!
